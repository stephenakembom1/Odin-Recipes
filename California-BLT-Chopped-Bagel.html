California BLT Chopped Bagel

To make this California BLT chopped bagel, you'll chop bacon, tomato, avocado,
and lettuce into a smear of chive cream cheese, then sandwich it into
an everything bagel. It's quick, easy, and so, so good.

Ingredients

  • 1 everything bagel
  • 2 ounces chive and onion cream cheese, softened
  • 3 slices crisp cooked bacon
  • 2 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 1 slice tomato
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 avocado, diced

Steps

  1. Toast bagel until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, slightly spread cream cheese on a cutting board. Add bacon, lettuce,
    and tomato on top. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and chop mixture until
    chunky and well incorporated. Add avocado and chop 5 to 7 more times.
  3. Gather mixture together; scrape up with a spatula or bench scraper. Spread
    mixture onto bottom of toasted bagel and place top piece on top. Serve
    immediately.
