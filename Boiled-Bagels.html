Boiled Bagels

For a glossier surface, place raised bagels on an ungreased baking sheet prior to
boiling them. Broil them, 5 inches from heat, for 1 to 1 1/2 minutes on each side.
Then put them into the hot water to be boiled as above. Note: do not bake
broiled bagels as long as non-broiled ones, 25 minutes should be long enough.

Ingredients

  • 4 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, divided, or as needed
  • 2 (.25 ounce) packages active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar, divided
  • 1 tablespoon salt
