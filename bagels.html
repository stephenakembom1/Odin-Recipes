Hot Honey Butter Cream Cheese Stuffed Bagel

This hot honey butter cream cheese stuffed bagel is a twist

On the viral air fryer stuffed bagel, with tangy cream cheese inside

And a hot honey caramelized outside

Ingredients

  • 1 bagel
  • 1 ounce cream cheese, cut into 8 slices
  • 1 tablespoon hot honey or regular honey, plus more for drizzle
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • flaky salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (199 degrees C) according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Make 8 small cuts in the bagel, taking care not to cut all the way through,
    to create small openings. Add a piece of cream cheese in each crevice around the bagel.
    Combine honey and melted butter in a small dish and coat the stuffed bagel liberally. Sprinkle with salt.
  3. Place bagel in the air fryer until golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes. Drizzle with more honey if desired.
